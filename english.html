Women in Computer Science

Statistics About Women in Computer Science

Only 20% of computer science degrees earned in 2022 were by women.

Computer Science has one of the lowest pay gaps between men and women, with women earning 94% of what men earn.

Almost 80% of computer scientist are men.

Reasons Why More Women Shoud Get Into Computer Science

Women should take more interest in tech fields because of the flexibility with scheduling.

Tech companies need more women programmers because studies show diverse teams are more innovative.

Through computer science women can make positive changes in the world.

Women tend to pay more attention to detail which can help them greatly in their ability to program
